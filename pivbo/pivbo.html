New Simulation

Skip to first bar with data available
Shifts the start bar forward so the MA line is visible from bar 1. Previous bars still show as context. No-op if your chosen start date is already past the MA's warmup.
Advanced — Flexed opening-bar SL
When on, the SL check on the bar a trade enters uses only the bar's close, and any fill is capped at the SL price. Addresses the edge case where a bar opens below your SL (long), rallies to trigger your entry, dips back, then closes above — strict mode would report an impossible stop-out at the open.

Duel Mode

Pick the duel's constraints. A random ticker matching them will be selected, and the sim will run for exactly the chosen number of years (252 trading bars per year). Eligible tickers are those with enough history to cover that range plus any MA warmup.

The Duel Pact
This is a friendly duel, not a cage match. The market has already humbled everyone here. No need to pile on.

You both win the moment you hit Start. You're about to get sharper at the stuff that actually matters: reading bars in real time, resisting FOMO, not chasing, not revenging, not freezing.

So: no peeking at the live chart in another tab. No googling the ticker. No asking your dog what happens next.

Trade the bars you're given. Learn from the market together with your opponent, and high-five each other afterward.

PivBO: Pivot BreakOutbeta
PivBO is “coffeeware”: completely FREE and open source, running purely on donated caffeine
Study momentum setups on historical US equities. Replay any ticker one bar at a time, manage positions with full risk discipline, and challenge a friend to a timed head-to-head duel.
Chart
Browse any ticker, any timeframe. Configurable moving averages, ADR and ATR overlays, secondary chart for market context, drawing tools, log/linear scale.
Simulator
Replay charts bar-by-bar. Place market and limit orders, set stop losses, take partials, track R-multiples and batting average. Save, resume, and analyze every session.
Duel Mode
Head-to-head practice against a friend. Same ticker, same bars, synchronized countdowns. Trade blind or visible. Side-by-side comparison report at the end.
Data
Browse your local chart data. Add new tickers, download fresh bars from Yahoo Finance, bulk-update the whole library, and check coverage ranges per symbol.

New simulation defaults

When a new simulation starts, the New Simulation modal's Flexed opening-bar SL checkbox is pre-set to this value. You can still flip it per sim. Recommended on: prevents impossible fills on bars where price gaps past your SL, rallies to trigger entry, and closes back inside.
